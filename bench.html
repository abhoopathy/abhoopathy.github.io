Bench

UI/UX
Sketch
Infographics

I was tasked with re-imagining the infographics and dashboards for Bench, a popular accounting service for small to medium sized businesses.

As this was my first intensive infographic project, I did extensive research on infographic best practices, color palettes, and other facets of information design.

I ended up developing a unique interaction concept, which melded a navigational menu with a timeseries graph.
